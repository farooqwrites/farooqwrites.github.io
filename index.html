About Me

Farooq Sattar

Date of Birth: 3 March 2004

Hometown: Upper Dir, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan

Education: BS English Literature

University: National University of Modern Languages (NUML), Peshawar Campus

Profession: Author | Novelist | Poet

Website: Farooq Writes

I am Farooq Sattar, a BS English Literature student at the National University of Modern Languages (NUML), Peshawar Campus, originally from Upper Dir, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. I am an aspiring author, novelist, and poet with a deep passion for literature and creative writing. Through Farooq Writes, I strive to share meaningful stories, thought-provoking poetry, and inspiring ideas that connect with readers and celebrate the power of words.

🌟 The Journey Behind Farooq Writes

👶 2004 – The Beginning

I was born on 3 March 2004 in Upper Dir, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan.

🎓 School & College

I completed my Matriculation and Intermediate in Upper Dir. During my school years, I developed a love for literature, public speaking, poetry, and creative writing.

🥇 2019

First Position in the Provincial Urdu Speech Competition at Hira School & College, Almas, Upper Dir.

🎓 2024

I started my BS English Literature at NUML Peshawar and secured Third Position in the university Urdu Speech Competition.

🥈 2026

I secured Second Position in the NUML Urdu Speech Competition.

✍️ A Dream That Continues

When I started writing poetry, short stories, and my novel, not everyone believed in my dream. Instead of giving up, I kept writing with patience and determination.

One of my friends supported and motivated me throughout this journey. I will always remain grateful for that encouragement.

Today I continue writing Ishq-e-Bemanzil, expected to be published in 2027.
