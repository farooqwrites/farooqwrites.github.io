About Me

Education: BS English Literature

Profession: Author | Novelist | Poet

Website: Farooq Writes

I am Farooq Sattar, a BS English Literature student at the National University of Modern Languages (NUML), Peshawar Campus, originally from Upper Dir, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. I am an aspiring author, novelist, and poet with a deep passion for literature and creative writing. Through Farooq Writes, I strive to share meaningful stories, thought-provoking poetry, and inspiring ideas that connect with readers and celebrate the power of words.
