"Ishq-e-Bemanzil" is an upcoming Urdu novel that explores love, hope, sacrifice, emotions, and the journey of self-discovery. The novel reflects human feelings and the beauty of unfinished dreams.

Author's Note

Every novel begins with a dream. "Ishq-e-Bemanzil" is not just a story; it is a part of my journey as a writer. I hope this book finds a place in the hearts of its readers.
